<h2>Translating amorphous ideas
into digital reality</h2>

Since young, I have been a native to the digital world, thus I naturally digest a significant amount of media, from gaming to reading articles on LinkedIn to watching food travel documentaries on YouTube. After I graduated with my degree in Business Marketing and working at a nonprofit, I realized that something was lacking. I fell in love with coding, and I am motivated to keep learning to build things that matters. My passion for a career in the tech industry combined with an interest for startups made me immerse myself in the world of Design and Front End Development. I came to startup institute because I want to love my career and work with passion.

  • Define
    I typically start every project by defining the goal and audience of the website, whether that is a portfolio, a marketing site, or an agency site.
  • Plan
    I tackle planning using a content-first approach. Planning involves choosing and creating content, including text, images, videos, icons, fonts, etc. I then start thinking about other site features such as navigation, and structure the site as a whole.
  • Design
    I usually start by sketching mockups to get the ideas out of my head. This helps me explore additional ideas and create a visual concept to quickly retain my ideas. For bigger projects I would create medium to high fidelity prototypes before developing.
  • Develop
    Code!
  • Optimize
    To deliver the best user experience from a developer's perspective, I like to optimize the site in terms of site speed, and implement SEO best practices.
